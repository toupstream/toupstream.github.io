Dub

Replacing the background music changes the meaning of the image immediately. I was impressed by a virus video. It’s a stormy night, a clerk in a drench business suit is waiting for the street light with no cars; while smoking. The song Going Home by Kenny G is playing in the background, drastically increase the tension. By building a tool to switch between images and music limitlessly, I’m exploring how this juxtaposition is intensifying the meaning of images.
