Interdimensional

Inter-dimensional walkers travel through unpredictable dimensional space, recording thoughts and experiences, collecting materials in this collective log, which is called Interdimensional.

Wired thoughts always come out of my mind. Since we know very little about time and space, it would be possible that the multidimensional encounter happens frequently. A spices-wise distinction in two dimensions could’ve happened because they accidentally appeared in front of me while I’m sneezing. As these thoughts accumulated, I need a proper format to present them other than only texts. That’s when I started to experiment with interactive storytelling.

Processing is a great tool for me to start coding and have fun. I started with very basic ideas, like making texts move, disrupting interactive experiences we are so used to and etc. During the study in Bergen, I had tried to narrate projects from my classes under this context, and they turn out becoming part of it. I had also tried to establish a certain style, associating with digital media and scientific knowledge.
