Digital Leak Responding

Digital Leak Responding is a design fiction built upon a digital incubator, for people to discover ideas about the digital spices. These organisms live in the virtual space, evolving their embedded artificial intelligence by interacting with the surroundings. Such an adaptive strategy provides them the potential to emerge collaboration skills, an intangible indication of a primitive society.

This project was derived from the impressive documentary presented by NHK(2018). It’s focused on a group of teenagers in China, who rather live in poverty than work in intensive factories like Foxconn. Crowded dormitory and cheap supply, their terrible life situation is remote but relatable.

If there is an alternative society that can engage more diversity, how will it look? Collaboration and tool use presents in lately artificial intelligence(OpenAI, 2019). Accounting for this, I decided to experiment with the possibility of generative society, a computational emergent social system. It’s a computer simulation for agents to interact with each other and the environments.
