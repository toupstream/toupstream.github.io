Hyperloading

Hyper loading is a recreational digital zine dedicating to cosmic secrets. Issue no.1 is Black Box, referring to a celestial contraption that was salvaged from deep space, which is considered as a black box by experts. This publication is basically an elaborative manual of fictional cosmic truth.
*PS: Discovering the hidden message possibly by interacting with elements on the page.

Have I ever been upset by ebooks? Hell yeah. Why on earth did they choose to imitate paper books out of such possibilities brought by computers and the internet? A legitimate digital zine is ought to be discovered. I’d like to experiment with the digital medium to find out some of the possibilities. With the interest of science fiction, a black box from the universe became the subject of the initial issue.

I was obsessed with the word hyper loading and pasted it on a barber’s pole, resulting in a dynamic logo for the digital zine. “A black box salvaged from deep space.”; departing from this simple idea, I started to create some plausible theories about the universe. To maintain the simpleness of the landing page, I later hid all the information into the celestial contraption and set up entrance and interactions accordingly.
