Climate Log

After collaboration with scientific researcher Clemens, I wrote down my thoughts and ideas about climate change in this log. Although it’s designed for interactive media, to engage audiences with rich experiences.

During the workshop of Art&Science, I’ve been paired up with Clemens, a climate researcher in the university. We’ve been asked to contribute to a blog with our thoughts and interviews so I decided to keep messing up with pictures and texts, to make some different results.

I tried to develop a more structural process this time. I started with sketches, developing a visual style after the function and interaction experiences are decided. The last part of this sketch was improved a lot by Clemens's idea. He thought those random lines were the record of temperature in different years and I think it’s definitely a great idea to follow.
