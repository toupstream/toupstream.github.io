Hyper Loading

Hyper Loading is a recreational zine about some mysteries which are beyond the sustentation and understanding of human being. Issue 1 is about an intreactive black box picked from hyperspace, which claims that it is coming and delivering some messages to human. The black box is made up of four parts: Laws of playing, Circuit of Background, Mechanism of Rendering and Escaping Pot. By using mouse and keyboard to play around with the page, you could find these messages through noticing the hidden hints.

超负荷是一本娱乐志，专注于那些超出人类承受与理解范围的谜题。 创刊号“黑匣子”为大家带来一个打捞自宇宙深处的不明装置，携带着一些“所谓”致全人类的奇怪信息。 “黑匣子”总共有四个部分：游戏定律、后台线路、渲染机制和逃生舱。通过使用鼠标键盘与页面进行互动，留意隐藏的线索，可以找到这些内容。

超负荷

* 由于图片数量较多，载入过程会花费较长时间，请耐心等待。

* Because of the large number of images, the loading process will take a while, please be patient.

iDwa TV

iDwa TV is an imaginary scene consists with an interactive ambiguous character and some hyperlinks. It can be treated as the actualization of my “id”(refer to Freud). It’s just like peeking my mind through a TV, where my whimsicality bursts out. The links refer to webpages of articles or other stuff which I’m interested in, which makes it also works like an information ferry, shows the funny messages I recommended in an heuristic way.

These messages are changeable, which means that I’ve built a frame. It’s kind of a scheme designed for gathering messages, or it could end up being an endoscope of your mind, by introducing the user’s data of the audience.

Interdimensional

It’s a story about inter-dimensional walker, recording his various experiences in different dimensions. You can arrive certain location through space folding machine, or just start from the beginning. Because of the limitation of technology, the materials across dimensions can only carry fragmentary information; therefore, besides the walker himself, anyone else has to use these information to deduce the original story.

这是一个关于维度旅行者的故事，记录了他在不同维度中各种各样的见闻。你可以通过空间折叠器前往指定的坐标，也可以从头开始。因为技术有限，穿越了维度的物质只能承载碎片化的信息，所以恐怕除了旅行者本人外，其他人大概只能通过这些片段去推断故事的原貌。

跨维度接触

* 这些信息零落且不完整，需要较长的载入时间，请耐心等待。在观看过程中，请多使用鼠标进行交互以免遗漏被维度压缩的信息。

* These fragmentary and incomplete pieces of information need longer period of time to load, please wait patiently. While watching, please use your mouse to interact with the information, in case to avoid missing information.

如果我们可以通过游戏的方法来读书，学习，保持锻炼甚至是增强自己的意志力，会怎么样？虽然听起来很不着边际，但这具有理论上的可能。

Hu Jiu

White spirit in the market focuses on older people. Creating a new brand by redesigning the packaging, I used modern layouts and compositions to rearrange traditional Chinese painting elements. I related the different flavors of white spirit from different characters in a famous story, "All men are brothers".

市面上的白酒品牌对于年轻人来说太过老气，我尝试从包装设计入手，重新设计一个年轻化的白酒品牌。我用现代化的版式来组织传统的国画元素，将酒的口味和水浒传里不同性格的角色联系起来，设计出这款白酒包装。

浒酒

* 包装设计，2016中南星奖一等奖。

* Packaging Design, first prize of Star of Central South, 2016.
